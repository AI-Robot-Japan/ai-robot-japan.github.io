FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Can beginners give talks?

Yes. Beyond cutting-edge research, we welcome stories of implementation struggles, experiments you've tried, and short lightning talks. Sharing the process of building and running is exactly what we value.

Can individuals and companies both apply?

Both are welcome — from individual talks and participation to corporate venue hosting and sponsorship. Reach out about whatever fits your involvement.

Are there any costs?

Speaking, attending, and joining the organizing team are free. For co-hosting and sponsorship, let's discuss the details case by case.

Can you hold events outside Tokyo?

Yes. With a venue, we are planning events nationwide, including Osaka, Kyoto, and Nagoya. We welcome inquiries about hosting in your region.

Can I join online?

Because we emphasize hands-on demos, events are mainly in person, but we also consider online or hybrid formats for some. Just ask.
